Job-Specific Skills & Technologies for an OSINT Analyst

    Programming & Scripting: Python (BeautifulSoup, Scrapy), JavaScript, Bash

    Web Scraping & Automation: Selenium, Puppeteer, Scrapy

    Threat Intelligence & Investigation: Maltego, MISP, VirusTotal, OpenCTI

Cybersecurity & Ethical Hacking

    Penetration Testing: Kali Linux, Metasploit, Burp Suite

    Digital Forensics: Autopsy, Volatility, FTK Imager

    Threat Hunting & Risk Assessment: MITRE ATT&CK, RiskIQ

Soft Skills

    Analytical thinking & problem-solving

    Attention to detail & pattern recognition

    Ethical data gathering & compliance with legal frameworks

    Effective communication & reporting findings
